- Chemistry, Chemical Engineering, or Physics
- Computer Science with interest in scientific computing
- Materials Science with computational focus

Experience with molecular simulations, Python programming, or machine learning is a plus but not required. Please contact Prof. Yu at jiwoongs1492@ajou.ac.kr with your CV and a brief statement of research interests.
