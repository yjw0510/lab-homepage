The Multiscale Molecular Computational Chemistry Lab (Yu Lab) opens at Ajou University in March 2026, housed in the School of Frontier Sciences and the Department of Chemistry. The lab is led by Prof. Ji Woong Yu, who previously developed machine learning force fields for aqueous systems at the Korea Institute for Advanced Study (KIAS) and studied nanoparticle superlattice assembly during his doctorate at Seoul National University.

We are recruiting graduate students with backgrounds in chemistry, physics, or computer science. See the Contact page for details.
